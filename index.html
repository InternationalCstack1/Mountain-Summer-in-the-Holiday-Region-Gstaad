Mountain Summer in the Holiday Region Gstaad

Gstaad – a picturesque region straight out of a picture book: With 11,000 cows, over 80 Alps and 10 charming chalet villages, it offers an authentic and family-friendly vacation region. Here, tradition, nature and adventure merge into a unique experience.

Family Hike “Alpenzauber- Weg”

The Alpenzauberweg is an exciting, easy hike that will delight young and old alike (approx. 2.5 hours). It leads from the Rinderberg over the Hornberg to the Horneggli and offers exciting discoveries along the way. The hiking trail starts in Zweisimmen, where the gondola takes you up the Rinderberg in comfort. The first highlight awaits the children there: olga’s cowshed, the indoor experience on the Rinderberg. Here they can playfully learn where milk and cheese come from.

The hike is accompanied by the family mascot “Saani”, the little white goat, and her friends. They tell exciting stories about alpine living and impart interesting knowledge. An unforgettable nature experience for the whole family!

Mountain Waters & Outdoor Swimming Pools

Magnificent lakes, streams and waterfalls in the midst of nature refresh active guests and summer lovers alike. Each place has its own unique charm and immediately captivates visitors. “Lauenensee” lies in the middle of a nature reserve and is an idyllic excursion destination for nature lovers and those seeking relaxation. The “Seebergsee” in the regional nature park lies on the plateau (1831 meters above sea level) between the Diemtigtal valley and the Simmental valley. The idyllic “Arnensee “is a jewel in the Saanenland: swimming in small bays, fishing, barbecuing at well- equipped fireplaces and various hikes offer pure summer enjoyment away from the hustle and bustle.

Summer activities in our mountains | Gstaad holiday region

With the outdoor pool in Saanen Zweisimmen or the “Piscine” in the Gstaad Palace, there is also the opportunity to cool off in an outdoor pool. With slides or a diving board, children can have a great time.

An Experience for the Whole Family

In summer, saani’s family program provides plenty of fun, games and adventure. It offers nature-related experiences for the whole family, for example a guided tour of the cheese grotto or an exploration of the “Lauenensee” nature reserve.

Participation in the saani’s family program is free with the Gstaad Card, but advance booking is required.
